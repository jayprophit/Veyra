MONETISATION DECISION MATRIX
================================================================================

For Priority #1 Project:

QUESTION 1: Is this a consumer app or B2B tool?
□ Consumer → Consider: Paid app, subscription, freemium
□ B2B → Consider: API access, per-seat pricing, enterprise

QUESTION 2: Does it require ongoing costs to operate?
□ Yes, significant → Price must cover: £[monthly cost] × 3 = £_____/month minimum
□ Minimal costs → Price based on value: £_____

QUESTION 3: What would I pay for this?
□ £1-5 → Volume strategy (aim for 1000+ users)
□ £5-20 → Standard pricing (aim for 100-500 users)
□ £20-100 → Premium (aim for 20-100 users)
□ £100+ → Enterprise (aim for 5-20 clients)

QUESTION 4: What are competitors charging?
□ Research 3 competitors: £_____, £_____, £_____
