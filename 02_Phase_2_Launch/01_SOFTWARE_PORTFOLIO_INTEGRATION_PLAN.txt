PART 5: TAX & LEGAL CONSIDERATIONS
================================================================================

SOFTWARE REVENUE TAXATION:
--------------------------

Activity                    | Tax Type      | Threshold       | Rate
----------------------------|---------------|-----------------|-------
App Store sales (Apple/Google)| Income Tax  | >£1,000/year    | 20% (basic)
Direct website sales          | Income Tax    | >£1,000/year    | 20%
B2B API subscriptions         | Income Tax    | >£1,000/year    | 20%
Software license fees         | Income Tax    | >£1,000/year    | 20%
Total turnover >£90,000       | + VAT         | £90,000+        | 20%

REGISTRATION REQUIREMENTS:
--------------------------
Sole Trader (Recommended for Years 1-3):
• Register for Self Assessment by 5 October after exceeding £1,000
• File annually, pay by 31 January
• Allowable expenses reduce taxable profit

Limited Company (Recommended for Years 4+):
• Incorporate at Companies House (£12)
• Corporation Tax: 19-25% on profits
• More tax-efficient for higher revenues
• Required for B2B enterprise sales

ALLOWABLE EXPENSES (Software Development):
-------------------------------------------
DEDUCT from taxable income:
• Domain names and hosting
• App Store developer fees (£79/yr Apple, £25 Google)
• Software licenses (IDE, design tools)
• Development hardware (test devices)
• Home office portion (if applicable)
• Professional services (legal, accounting)
• Training and courses

Keep ALL receipts. Use accounting software (FreeAgent, Xero, QuickBooks).

VAT CONSIDERATIONS:
-------------------
• App Store sales: Apple/Google handle VAT collection in most regions
• You receive net proceeds, still report as income
• B2B EU sales: Reverse charge mechanism
• Direct sales >£90,000/year: Register for VAT within 30 days

OPEN SOURCE IMPLICATIONS:
-------------------------
If using GPL code in commercial product:
• Must release your modifications publicly
• Cannot keep proprietary if GPL components present

If using MIT/Apache/BSD code:
• Can keep proprietary
• Must include license attribution

Document EVERY dependency and its license.
