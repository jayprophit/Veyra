GUARDRAILS FOR AI AUTONOMY
============================

Essential Limits (Never Disable):
----------------------------------
1. MAXIMUM SINGLE ASSET ALLOCATION
   - Never exceed 25% in any single asset
   - Even if AI suggests "amazing opportunity"
   - Prevents catastrophic loss from one failure

2. LIQUIDITY MINIMUMS
   - Always maintain 3-6 months expenses in liquid form
   - AI cannot invest emergency fund
   - Prevents cash flow crises

3. TAX COMPLIANCE
   - AI cannot execute trades that violate tax rules
   - Wash sale rule (US) / Bed & breakfast (UK) enforced
   - Annual allowance limits tracked (ISA, SIPP, etc.)

4. CRYPTO LIMITS
   - Maximum 20% total portfolio in crypto
   - No memecoins or unaudited contracts
   - Hardware wallet required for large holdings

5. APPROVAL REQUIREMENTS
   - Trades >£10,000 require human approval
   - New asset classes require review
   - Changes to guardrails need 24-hour confirmation

6. AUDIT TRAIL
   - Every AI decision logged with reasoning
   - Monthly human review mandatory
   - Annual third-party audit (optional but recommended)


OVERSIGHT FRAMEWORK
===================

Daily (2 minutes):
• Check AI alert summary email
• Approve/reject flagged transactions
• Review any anomalies

Weekly (15 minutes):

DATA SECURITY BEST PRACTICES
=============================

1. API KEY MANAGEMENT
   - Use read-only keys where possible
   - Whitelist IP addresses
   - Rotate keys quarterly
   - Store in encrypted vault (1Password, Bitwarden)
   - Never share keys in emails or chat

2. ACCOUNT ACCESS
   - Enable 2FA on all accounts
   - Use unique passwords (password manager)
   - Review connected apps monthly
   - Revoke unused connections

3. AI TOOL SELECTION
   - Prefer SOC 2 compliant tools
   - Review privacy policies
   - EU-hosted for GDPR compliance (if in EU)
   - Self-hosted options for sensitive data

4. ANONYMIZATION
   - Use account IDs, not names
   - Don't share actual account numbers with AI
   - Redact sensitive data in logs
   - Encrypt backups

5. INCIDENT RESPONSE
   - Document all AI permissions
   - Monitor for unauthorized access
   - Plan for data breach (what AI knows)
   - Insurance for cyber incidents
