PART 1: UK CREDIT SCORE SYSTEM
================================================================================

UNDERSTANDING YOUR CREDIT SCORE
--------------------------------
The UK has THREE Credit Reference Agencies (CRAs), each with different scoring:

Agency        | Score Range  | Poor       | Fair       | Good        | Excellent
--------------|--------------|------------|------------|-------------|------------
Experian      | 0-999        | 0-560      | 561-720    | 721-880     | 881-999
Equifax       | 0-1000       | 0-438      | 439-530    | 531-810     | 811-1000
TransUnion    | 0-710        | 0-550      | 551-565    | 566-603     | 604-710

TARGET SCORES:
--------------
• Minimum for mortgages: "Good" rating (Experian 720+, Equifax 530+, TransUnion 566+)
• Best mortgage rates: "Excellent" (Experian 881+, Equifax 811+, TransUnion 604+)
• Business loans: Strong score above 750/1000 gets best rates

WHY IT MATTERS FOR YOUR PLAN:
-----------------------------
• Mortgage for first home (Help to Buy ISA): Need good score for best rates
• Buy-to-let property investment (Phase 5): Excellent score = lower interest
• Business loans for equipment (Tier 3-4): Score affects approval and rates
• Commercial mortgage (industrial unit): Business AND personal credit checked
• Credit cards for business: Higher limits with better score

PART 4: DRIVING LICENSE POINTS MANAGEMENT
================================================================================

UNDERSTANDING THE UK POINTS SYSTEM
-----------------------------------
• Penalty points (endorsements) added for motoring offences
• Points stay on license for 4-11 years (depending on offence)
• Accumulate 12+ points in 3 years = automatic disqualification ("totting up")
• New drivers: 6+ points = license revoked (retake tests)

COMMON OFFENCES & POINTS:
--------------------------
Offence                  | Code   | Points | Duration
-------------------------|--------|--------|----------
Speeding (minor)         | SP30   | 3      | 4 years
Speeding (major)         | SP50   | 6      | 4 years
Using mobile phone       | CU80   | 6      | 4 years
Careless driving         | CD10   | 3-9    | 4 years
No insurance             | IN10   | 6-8    | 4 years
Drink driving            | DR10   | 3-11   | 11 years
Dangerous driving        | DD40   | 3-11   | 4 years

CHECK YOUR POINTS:
------------------
☐ Online: gov.uk/view-driving-licence
☐ Need: Driving license number, National Insurance number, postcode
☐ Shows: Current points, endorsements, expiry dates

COST: FREE
FREQUENCY: Check every 6 months


PROTECTING YOUR LICENSE (ESSENTIAL FOR WORK):
---------------------------------------------
You drive to work (300 miles/week)
Losing license = losing income

DEFENSIVE DRIVING HABITS:
-------------------------
[ ] Speed Awareness
   • Use Waze or Google Maps (shows speed cameras)
   • Cruise control on motorways (prevents creeping over)
   • Check speedometer every 30 seconds in towns

[ ] Mobile Phone
   • NEVER touch phone while driving (6 points)
   • Use hands-free only if essential
   • Better: Pull over safely if call needed

[ ] Documentation
   • Insurance: Check renewal date (calendar alert 30 days before)
   • MOT: Check expiry (calendar alert 30 days before)
   • Tax: Auto-renew via Direct Debit
   • Keep all documents in car

[ ] Vehicle Condition
   • Check tyre tread monthly (1.6mm minimum)
   • Check lights weekly
   • Check wipers, brakes, fluids
   • Fix issues immediately

[ ] Speed Camera Locations
   • Learn regular route cameras
   • Waze alerts for mobile cameras
   • Assume camera on any motorway

POINTS REMOVAL:
---------------
• After 4 years: Points removed from license
• After 3 years: Points don't count toward "totting up" ban
• Can take speed awareness course (once every 3 years) to avoid points
