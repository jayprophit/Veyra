PHASE SUITABILITY: 4-5
Startup Cost: £5,000-£100,000+
Expected Returns: Highly variable (-100% to 1000%+)
Risk: Very High
Alignment: 7/10 (startup ecosystem knowledge)

• No guarantee of returns

• Phase 5+: As you understand business better, evaluate opportunities

What: Life insurance bond for tax deferral
Benefit: No tax on gains until withdrawal
Use Case: Higher rate taxpayers in accumulation phase
Providers: Old Mutual International, Prudential, Canada Life

PHASE SUITABILITY: 4-5
Startup Cost: £50,000+ (typically)
Expected Returns: Market-linked (fees apply)
Risk: Low-Medium
Alignment: 5/10

HOW IT WORKS:
• Invest lump sum in offshore bond
• Gains roll up gross (no tax)
• Withdraw 5% annually tax-deferred
• Taxable only on full surrender or exceeding 5%
• Useful for timing income in lower tax years

• High fees compared to direct investment
• Complex product, seek advice
