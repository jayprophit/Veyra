TAX OPTIMIZATION BY CAPITAL TIER
----------------------------------
TIER 1 (£1-£500):
• Use ISA wrapper for all investments where possible
• Claim Trading Allowance (£1,000) for side hustles
• Premium Bonds = completely tax-free

TIER 2 (£500-£5,000):
• Max out ISA allowance (£20,000/year) before taxable accounts
• Use IFISA wrapper for P2P lending
• EIS investments = 30% tax relief + CGT exemption

TIER 3 (£5,000-£50,000):
• SEIS investments = 50% tax relief (highest available)
• VCT investments = 20% tax relief + tax-free dividends
• Consider Ltd Company structure if business income >£50k/year

TIER 4 (£50,000-£500,000):
• Ltd Company + salary/dividend split optimizes tax
• HMO property via Ltd Company (Section 24 workaround)
• Multiple VCTs for ongoing tax relief

TIER 5 (£500,000+):
• Family Investment Company (FIC) for wealth preservation
• Offshore structures (legitimate, not evasion)
• Residency/Citizenship by investment for global mobility
