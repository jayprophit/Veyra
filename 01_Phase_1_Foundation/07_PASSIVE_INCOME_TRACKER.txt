HOW TO USE THIS DOCUMENT
================================================================================

PURPOSE:
--------
This is your living inventory of passive income opportunities. Unlike fixed plans,
this tracker is designed to evolve as you:
• Start new income streams (change status to 🟢 Active)
• Pause opportunities temporarily (change to 🟡 Paused)  
• Retire what no longer works (change to 🔴 Retired)
• Prioritize top opportunities (mark as ⭐ Priority)
• Add new discoveries to the list

STATUS LEGEND:
--------------
⚪ Planned   → Intend to pursue in the future (review quarterly)
🟢 Active    → Currently generating income (track monthly)
🟡 Paused    → Temporarily on hold (review quarterly)
🔴 Retired   → No longer pursued (keep for records)
⭐ Priority  → Top 3-5 focus areas (dedicate weekly time)

INTEGRATION:
------------
Copy the table structure below into your Airtable or Google Sheets:
• Table Name: Passive_Income_Opportunities
• Link to: Global Financial Intelligence System
• View Filters: Active, Priority, Phase 2, etc.
