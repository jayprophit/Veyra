CHINA SYSTEM EXPLANATION:

**Private Financial Scoring (What most people use):**
- Sesame Credit (Ant Group/Alipay): 350-950

- Not government-run, not punishment-based

- Central bank credit database
- No public score number
- Records: Loans, credit cards, defaults

- 2026 focus: Corporate credit expansion

- NOT a single score for citizens
- TWO systems: Corporate compliance + Individual court judgments
- Most people never affected
- Punishments: For serious legal violations only

Key 2026 Updates:
- Sesame Credit expanded to more services
- Corporate social credit: Stricter enforcement
- Personal PBOC: Better data integration
