WHY AUTOMATE?
-------------
• Removes emotion from investing

--------------------------------------

• Tap "AutoInvest"

  - Frequency: Monthly
  - Amount: £20
  - Day: 1st of month (or day after payday)
• Confirm AutoInvest setup

WHAT HAPPENS NEXT:
• Every month on your chosen date
• Trading 212 automatically buys £20 of VWRP
• Uses funds from your Trading 212 cash balance
• You'll get notification of each purchase
• Shares are added to your holding

ACTION 3.2: Ensure Cash Balance Available

• You need £20 available in Trading 212 each month

  1. Set standing order from bank to Trading 212 (£20/month)

• Set up alongside your emergency fund standing order
• Day after payday: £200 to emergency, £25 to Trading 212, bills to bills account

STEP 4: INFRASTRUCTURE ETF (Day 5-7)
================================================================================

WHAT IS INFRASTRUCTURE ETF?
---------------------------
Infrastructure ETFs invest in companies that own/operate:
• Toll roads and highways
• Airports and ports
• Electricity grids and utilities
• Pipelines and energy infrastructure
• Telecommunications towers
• Water systems

Why add this to VWRP?
✓ Different risk profile (physical assets)
✓ Often regulated/monopoly positions
✓ Long-term contracts (predictable cash flows)
✓ Inflation protection (regulated price increases)
✓ Lower volatility than tech stocks
✓ Complement to VWRP's broad exposure

RECOMMENDED INFRASTRUCTURE ETFs:
--------------------------------

1. iShares Global Infrastructure UCITS ETF (IGF)
   • Ticker: IGF
   • Domicile: US (slight tax complexity but manageable)
   • Holdings: 75+ infrastructure companies globally
   • Yield: ~3%
   • Fee: 0.46%

2. SPDR S&P Global Infrastructure ETF (GII)
   • Ticker: GII
   • Similar to IGF
   • Slightly different index methodology
   • Fee: 0.40%

3. iShares STOXX Europe 600 Utilities UCITS ETF (EXV1)
   • Ticker: EXV1
   • Europe-focused utilities
   • Lower fee: 0.20%
   • Less global diversification

For Trading 212 UK, search which tickers are available.
IGF or EXV1 likely available.

ACTION 4.1: Research Available Options
--------------------------------------
• In Trading 212, search "infrastructure"
• Look for:
  - UCITS ETFs (UK/EU compliant)
  - In GBP if possible (avoids FX fees)

• Read fact sheet (available in app)
• Check top holdings and geographic breakdown
