WHAT YOU CANNOT HAVE: LIFETIME ISA (LISA)
==========================================

LISA Rules:
• Must be aged 18-39 to OPEN
• Can contribute until age 50
• £4,000/year limit (vs £200/month Help to Buy)
• 25% government bonus
• Property cap: £450,000 nationwide
• Can use for first home OR retirement
• **25% PENALTY** for non-qualifying withdrawals

YOUR SITUATION:
