# Asset Intelligence

Private workstream for broad wealth-domain modeling.

## Planned Coverage

- public markets
- physical metals
- agriculture and food
- property and land
- private businesses and holding companies
- digital products, content, services, and recurring cashflow

## Boundary

This service should normalize valuation, ownership, liquidity, and provenance across domains without pretending that farmland, software revenue, and listed equities behave like the same asset.
