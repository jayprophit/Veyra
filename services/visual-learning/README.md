# Visual Learning

Starter workstream for multimodal research inputs.

## Active Now

- validated `VisualObservation` records
- deterministic frame ingestion
- tests for accepted and rejected metadata

## Not Active Yet

- live video capture
- OCR or chart detection
- model training
- trading decisions
- automated clip downloads

## Boundary

Visual observations are research inputs. They do not bypass canonical market data, policy checks, or human approval.
