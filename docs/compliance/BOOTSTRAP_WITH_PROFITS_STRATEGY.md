## Tax Implications (UK)

### Personal Trading Profits

```
Capital Gains Tax (CGT):
- Annual allowance: £3,000 (2024/25)
- Rate: 10% (basic) or 20% (higher rate)
- Applies to: Crypto, stocks, most assets

Trading Income (if "trader" status):
- Income Tax rates: 20%, 40%, 45%
- NICs: Class 4 (9% or 2%)
- Risk: HMRC may classify as trading not investing

Tax Efficiency Strategies:
1. Use ISA allowance (£20k/year) - Tax-free growth
2. Spread gains across tax years
3. Offset losses against gains
4. Consider Ltd company structure (Month 6+)
```

### Company Structure (Month 6)

```
Veyra Wealth Management Ltd

Benefits:
- Corporation Tax: 19-25% (vs 20-45% personal)
- Retain profits for FCA capital
- Limited liability protection
- Professional credibility

Structure:
- You: Director + Shareholder (100%)
- Later: Add investors (dilute to 75-90%)
- FCA: "Owner-controller" clear
```
