# Enterprise Deployment

Enterprise deployment is intentionally after the private pre-public product milestones and after the public-release hardening gate.

## Scope

- multi-environment infrastructure
- SSO and tenant isolation
- policy-managed secrets
- observability, backup, failover, and recovery drills
- compliance evidence and support processes

## Dependency

Do not treat this as active deployment work until the private product tracks are stable and the public-release gate has been satisfied.
